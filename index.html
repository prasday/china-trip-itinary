Cities on This Trip
🏙 Guangzhou (广州)
Capital of Guangdong province & one of China's largest trading hubs. Known as the "City of Flowers." Home to the famous Canton Fair — the largest trade fair in the world.
Currency
Chinese Yuan (CNY ¥)
~11.5 INR per ¥1
Language
Mandarin (official)
Cantonese (local)
Time Zone
CST (UTC+8)
+2:30 from IST
Weather (May–Jun)
28–34°C, Humid
Carry umbrella ☂
Must Try Food
Dim Sum, Wonton noodles, White Cut Chicken
Key Markets
Helong, Beijing Rd, Shangxiajiu, Zhanxi Rd
  • WeChat Pay / Alipay — essential for payments. Set up before you go.
  • Metro is cheap & fast — grab a Yangcheng Tong card (transit card).
  • VPN needed for Google, WhatsApp, Instagram. Install before arriving.
  • Most market vendors speak basic English; use Google Translate for negotiating.
  • Bargain hard — first price is always 3–5x the final price.
🏭 Lecong (乐从)
A town in Shunde District, Foshan — the furniture wholesale capital of the world. Lecong and nearby Longjiang host the largest concentration of furniture markets on Earth. Your main base.
Distance from GZ
~35 km southwest
~45 min by car
Key Market
Lecong Furniture City
Longjiang Market
Kungfu Market
Mixed wholesale goods
Open daily 8am–6pm
Nearby
Shunde — famous for food
Foshan city centre
  • Furniture markets are spread over several km — wear comfortable shoes.
  • Hire a local sourcing agent if buying large quantities — saves time & money.
  • Shunde cuisine is considered the best Cantonese food in the region — try it!
  • Take photos & note down supplier booth numbers for follow-up.
🤖 Shenzhen (深圳)
China's tech & innovation hub, bordering Hong Kong. World-famous for Huaqiang Bei — the largest electronics market on Earth. Also great for fashion at Dongmen. Modern, fast-paced city.
Distance from GZ
~140 km southeast
~1.5 hr by train/car
Huaqiang Bei
Electronics wholesale
Open 9am–6pm
Dongmen Market
Fashion & clothing
Open 10am–10pm
Window of World
Theme park / landmark
Open 10am–10pm
Weather
Similar to GZ
28–33°C, humid
Best for
Electronics, gadgets, fashion, accessories
  • Huaqiang Bei has multiple floors per building and dozens of buildings — allow full day.
  • Dongmen is best in the evening — more stalls open, lively atmosphere.
  • Dafen Oil Painting Village — great for unique affordable wall art to bring back home.
  • Shenzhen metro is world-class — very easy to navigate with Google Maps.
📦 General Travel Tips
SIM Card
Buy at airport — China Unicom recommended for foreigners
Transport
DiDi app (like Uber) for taxis. Metro in cities.
Shipping Goods
Use local freight agent in Lecong/GZ for bulk cargo home
Emergency
Police: 110 | Ambulance: 120 | Indian Embassy: +86-10-65321908
Must-Have Apps
WeChat, DiDi, Alipay, VPN, Google Translate (offline)
Carry Always
Passport, small cash ¥, power bank, umbrella
  • Download offline Google Maps for GZ, Shenzhen, Lecong before arriving.
  • Carry business cards — suppliers expect them and it makes follow-up easier.
  • Most restaurants have picture menus — easy to order without language.
  • Tap water is not safe to drink — buy bottled water.
  • Indian food available in Guangzhou's Xiaobei area if homesick!
